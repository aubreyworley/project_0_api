San Francisco Serenades

Our City. Our People. Our Stories.

San Francisco is a small, yet explosive 7x7 city. Within each district, each mile, each block lies unique culture, food, people and stories. It can be overwhelming, breathtaking, invigorating, eye opening, lonely, inclusive, scary, comforting, wretched, and magnificent all at the same time. It is a home for the artists, the musicians, the tourists, the hippies, the soul-searchers, the techies, the immigrants and everyone in between. It is a community working towards a greater future with individuals all fighting to survive, embrace, and love the magical City by the Bay. The constant, enthralling struggle to survive and thrive in the city, is what gives all San Franciscans a commonality no matter where they're from. San Francisco Serenades is a microblog that offers an online community platform for the people of SF to tell their stories: the good, the bad, the ugly, the beautiful.
